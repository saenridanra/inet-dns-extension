Detailed Description

DNSSimpleCache is a simple cache implementation with random eviction.

Once the maximum amount of records is reached, 10% of the lists of records are randomly evicted.

Author
Andreas Rain, Distributed Systems Group, University of Konstanz
Date
March 26, 2015
