Detailed Description

DNSTTLCache is a TTL based cached.

This means that once a record has outlived it's time to live, it is removed from the cache. If eviction is necessary due to a full cache, oldest entries are removed first, until 10% of the lists of records are removed.

Author
Andreas Rain, Distributed Systems Group, University of Konstanz
Date
March 26, 2015
